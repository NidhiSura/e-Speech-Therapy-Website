e-Speech Therapy

Innovation for good

e-Speech Therapy is a tool developed to help the hearing-imapired learn how to speak. It uses speech processing combined with AI algorithms to provide a seamless experience.

It consists of three steps:

  • Speech Sampling
  • Noise Removal
  • Forman Estimation (LPC)
